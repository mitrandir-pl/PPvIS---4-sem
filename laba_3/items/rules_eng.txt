Rules of the game Arkanoid

Purpose of the game
The goal of the Arkanoid game is to destroy all the colored panels with the ball as quickly as possible

How to play
With the help of the left and right buttons of the control platform
Use good bonuses for yourself bad bonuses for rivals
to quickly break all the bricks


Game bonuses
1 Burning ball is a ball that burns through all the blocks through
2 Platform Reducer is a surface area is reduced by 30 seconds
3 Platform Enlarger is an area of ​​​​the intended platform takes up 30 seconds more
4 Kettlebell slows down the action of the platform for 30 seconds
5 Plus blocks are release one line of blocks on your field
